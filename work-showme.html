showMe

Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL, AJAX

A movie-finder website created using a movie database API by TMDb.

Want to relax with a good movie but have no idea what to watch? This website can help you! Based on the user's mood and available time to watch a movie, showMe can show YOU a list of titles to suit your needs.

Visitors can use the service without needing to create an account. Members however have the added benefit of having their own profile where they can add movies to their watch/favourites list.
